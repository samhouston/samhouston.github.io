This seminar has the nefarious purpose of showing you in a quick and effective manner the caveats and joys of the Javascript Programming Language , brought to you by the SHACS Gaming Committee Chariman Ed de Luna

The content of this seminar trusts that you are well versed in the art of programming, so as to do away with the very basics and get to the brass tax about how to code in Javascript, its basic syntax (compared to Java), and the mysteries of prototypal inheritance, Javascript's most formidable weapon.


Join me on October 30th, and I'll be able to answer your questions live.
